History

The store was built by the married couple Urbano and Terisita Macabuhay way back August 2007.
Since there weren't that many competencies in the area, and also the demand was high, goats and sheeps are the chosen products that wants to be sold with.
Like any other stores, Terry & Jun Kambingan at Kainan started in a small business and encounters many struggles.
Because of hardworking and perserverance the business becomes stable and continues to serve good products.
